Inventory
Management System

A powerful desktop solution to manage stock, track products, monitor suppliers, handle purchases and generate reports — all from one organized system.

🖥️ Desktop Application 🐍 Python 🎨 PyQt5 🗄️ SQLite

🖥️ Project Overview

The Inventory Management System is a custom desktop application designed to simplify stock and product management for businesses. It helps track products, manage suppliers, monitor stock levels, record purchases and sales, generate reports, and maintain product-wise history.


This system reduces manual work, improves accuracy, and helps business owners keep full control of their daily inventory operations.

🔵 Business Problem

Businesses usually manage their inventory manually using notebooks or spreadsheets which leads to calculation errors, confusion in stock levels, difficulty in tracking products and no proper record-keeping.

🤖 Our Solution

We developed a simple, fast and reliable desktop software that digitizes the entire inventory workflow. Store all your data securely, track stock levels, manage suppliers, generate reports and take backups — all with a clean and easy-to-use interface.
