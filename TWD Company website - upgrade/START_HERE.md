## ⚡ Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### Option A: Fastest Deploy (2 minutes)

1. **Go to [netlify.com](https://netlify.com/)**
2. **Drag & drop** this entire folder
3. **Wait** for deployment (usually 30 seconds)
4. **Your website is LIVE!** ✨

Your URL will be something like: `https://[random-name].netlify.app`

### Option B: Add Contact Form (Additional 3 minutes)

For the contact form to send emails:

1. **Go to [emailjs.com](https://emailjs.com/)**
2. **Create account** and set up email service
3. **Copy your 3 credentials**
4. **Edit script.js** (line 10-13) and paste them
5. **Redeploy** to Netlify

**See EMAILJS_SETUP.md for detailed steps** ⚙️
