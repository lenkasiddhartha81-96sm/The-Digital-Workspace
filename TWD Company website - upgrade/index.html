About The Digital Workspace

Founded by Siddhartha Lenka, The Digital Workspace is a software development company based in Kolkata, India. We specialize in building modern websites, web applications, AI-powered solutions, and business automation tools that help businesses establish a strong digital presence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What technologies do you use?

+

We're technology agnostic and choose the best tools for each project. Our tech stack includes modern frameworks, cloud platforms, and databases. We specialize in full-stack development with expertise in multiple programming languages and platforms.

What's your typical project timeline?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A basic website might take 4-8 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions can take 3-6 months or more. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase and maintain regular communication throughout development.

Do you provide ongoing support?

+

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages. Whether it's bug fixes, security updates, feature additions, or performance optimization, we're here to ensure your solution continues to serve your business needs.

How do you handle project communication?

+

We maintain transparent communication through regular meetings, detailed progress reports, and collaborative tools. You'll have direct access to your project manager and can track progress in real-time through our project management systems.

What's your pricing model?

+

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, time & materials, and dedicated team augmentation. Each project is priced based on its unique requirements, scope, and complexity. We'll provide a detailed quote after understanding your needs.

How do you ensure security?

+

Security is paramount in everything we build. We follow industry best practices including secure coding standards, regular security audits, data encryption, secure authentication mechanisms, and compliance with relevant regulations like GDPR and data protection laws.
